2 · How to run each feed

TikTok
The growth engine

  • 4–7 posts / week — rewards volume + consistency.
  • Lo-fi beats polished. Wrap promos inside an animal moment.
  • Cute hook in the first second; trending sounds.
  • Pin 2–3 videos up top (one "book a walk," one "glamping soon").
  • Reply with video — free reach.
  • Geo-caption: "2 hrs from Nashville," "near Dale Hollow Lake."

Instagram
The booking storefront

  • 3–5 Reels / week + Stories daily.
  • Watermark-free — export from CapCut/originals, never a TikTok download.
  • Re-caption native; drop #fyp.
  • Build Story Highlights (0 today): Stay · Book Now · Glamping (soon) · Animals · Reviews · Events.
  • Carousels for the stay experience — most saves.
  • Always geo-tag Monroe, TN.

Facebook
Local & booking-intent

  • 3–4 posts / week — photo + story-driven captions + booking links.
  • Use Facebook Events for anything on-site.
  • Share into local / TN travel & RV groups — outsized free reach.
  • Reply to every comment & DM fast.
  • Fix the stale bio ("moving to TN" → arrived + glamping coming).

Film once, post everywhere — but re-caption per platform (short + emoji on IG/TikTok, a bit more narrative + a clear link on FB) and strip the watermark for IG. One farm walk = 10+ clips = a week across all three.
