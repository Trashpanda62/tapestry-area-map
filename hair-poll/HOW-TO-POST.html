Best times to post for a local farm audience: weekday 7–9am, 12–1pm, or 7–9pm, and weekend mornings.

Instagram

1) Feed post (the main event)

  • New post → select the announcement card first, then card_01 through card_10 (Instagram allows up to 10 images in a carousel — lead with the announce card, then Joey #1, etc.).
  • Paste the Launch caption (below). Post.
  • Make sure the poll link is in your bio (use your Linktree or paste the link directly in "Edit Profile → Website").

2) Stories (this is where votes come from)

  • Post 2–3 contestant cards to your Story. Add the Link sticker pointing to the poll link so people can tap straight through.
  • Fun option: use the Story poll sticker or "this or that" with two contestants at a time ("Joey vs The Diva — who's got better hair?") to build hype, then send them to the real poll via the link sticker.
  • Don't forget the goat — post her card with "she needs a NAME — vote at the link 🐐".

3) Reel (optional, biggest reach)

  • A quick 7–10s Reel flipping through the cards to trending audio, caption "Vote in bio 👆". Reels out-reach everything else right now.

Facebook

  • Create a post with the announcement card (or an album of all the cards). Paste the Launch caption.
  • Put the poll link right in the post text (Facebook shows it as a clickable preview — that's what the share image is for).
  • Pin the post to the top of your Page (⋯ menu → Pin to top).
  • Share it to relevant local groups (Livingston / Overton County community pages, homeschool & farm groups) — with permission where required.
  • Reply to every comment quickly the first few hours — engagement makes Facebook show it to more people.

TikTok

  • Make a photo slideshow (upload the cards) or a short video panning across them, set to a trending/again sound.
  • Caption: "Vote for the best hair on our farm 🦙 (link in bio) + help us name our goat 🐐". Add the hashtags.
  • TikTok doesn't do clickable links in captions well — say "link in bio" and pin a comment with the poll link.

Turn on shared, live voting (optional, ~2 min — one time)

Out of the box the poll counts votes per phone (great for a fun website vote). To make everyone see one shared live tally (and pool the write-in goat names), switch on the free Google backend:

  • Open C:\dev\hair-poll\Code.gs and follow the 6 setup steps at the top (script.google.com → paste → Deploy → Web app → Anyone → copy the /exec URL).
  • Send Claude that /exec URL and it'll switch the live poll to shared mode and re-deploy — or paste it into index.html just before </head> as shown in the file.

Not required to launch — you can go live today in per-device mode and turn on shared voting anytime.
